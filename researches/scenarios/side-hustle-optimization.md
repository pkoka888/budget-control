### Tax Optimization for Freelancers
```
CZECH FREELANCER TAX STRATEGY:

OSVČ REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS:
- Register as self-employed (OSVČ) for tax benefits
- Annual turnover threshold: Report if >15,000 CZK/year
- Social security: Voluntary payments for benefits
- Health insurance: Mandatory 13.5% of income

TAX OPTIMIZATION:
- Flat tax rate: 15% on income (vs. employment tax)
- Deductible expenses: Home office (20% of rent), software, hardware
- Quarterly payments: Plan for tax obligations
- Loss carry-forward: Utilize startup period losses

COMPLIANCE AUTOMATION:
- Expense tracking: Automatic categorization
- Tax reminders: Quarterly filing notifications
- Record keeping: Digital receipt storage
- Annual reporting: Simplified tax return preparation
```
